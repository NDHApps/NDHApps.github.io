About NDH Apps

NDH Apps was founded by Nick Hall in 2013 and the company is planning to release its first app, CamRub, in 2014. Nick is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Northwestern University in Evanston, IL and hopes to pursue a career in mobile development.

CamRub

CamRub is a new photography app which allows you to "rub," or draw on a camera view to capture portions of the image. Make unique drawings, create collages, or just play around to see what else you can come up with– your imagination is the only limit! When you're done, save your creations to your camera roll or share them on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Messages. Look for CamRub to hit the App Store in the near future once Apple approves the app for release.
