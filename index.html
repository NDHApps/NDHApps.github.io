CamRub

CamRub is a new photography app which allows you to "rub," or draw on a camera view to capture portions of the image. Make unique drawings, create collages, or just play around to see what else you can come up with– your imagination is the only limit! When you're done, save your creations to your camera roll or share them on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Messages.
